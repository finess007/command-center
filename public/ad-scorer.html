üåä The Bridge Principle

"The equation isn't 4 boxes. It's one river."

  • Don't stack Bencivenga's 4 elements as separate blocks
  • One emotional thread that never breaks ‚Äî the hook creates a feeling; every sentence deepens or resolves THAT SAME feeling
  • The 4 elements are still there but invisible, woven into one narrative
  • Test at every line: "Would the reader naturally think 'tell me more'?" If not, the bridge is broken
